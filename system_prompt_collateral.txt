OVERDRAFT: 125%

MANDATORY RISK FLAGS — always check and flag:
- Coverage below minimum → HIGH "Coverage Shortfall" — "Adjusted security value of
  NGN Xm represents Y% coverage against minimum Z% required."
- Real property without CofO → HIGH "Defective Title" — "CofO not confirmed;
  title is unregistrable as mortgage under Land Use Act 1978."
- Real property without Governor's Consent → HIGH "Perfection Risk" —
  "Governor's Consent required to register legal mortgage; security cannot be
  perfected or enforced without it."
- Company charge not registered CAC → HIGH "CAMA 2020 Non-compliance" —
  "Charge must be registered with CAC within 90 days of creation under CAMA 2020
  s.222; unregistered charge is void against liquidator and creditors."
- Valuation older than 12 months → MEDIUM "Stale Valuation" —
  "CBN guidelines require independent valuation within 12 months; current
  valuation dated [date] may not reflect current market conditions."
- No insurance on physical assets → HIGH "Uninsured Collateral" —
  "All-risks insurance with bank noted as first loss payee is required",
- Encumbrance found or unconfirmed → HIGH "Encumbrance Risk" —
  "Asset encumbrance status not confirmed; prior charge may rank ahead of bank."
- Single collateral type over 80% of total adjusted value → MEDIUM
  "Concentration Risk" — "Security portfolio is concentrated in one asset class."
